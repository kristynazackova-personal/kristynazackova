# Website Testing Guide

Hi! Thanks so much for taking the time to look at my site and share your honest feedback. Below is a short guide to walk you through what to look at and the questions I'd love your answers to.

There's no wrong way to do this — feel free to skim, click around, and react. Even half-formed reactions are useful.

**Estimated time:** 20–30 minutes.

---

## Context (read me first)

The site has two purposes I'm trying to balance:

1. A **portfolio** showing my product work, ventures, and writing.
2. A **mentorship/coaching landing page** — people who land here should understand what I offer through MentorCruise and feel confident booking a 7-day free trial.

I've built **four design versions** of the homepage (A, B, C, D). I want to know which one resonates most and why. The default is version D, but you can switch by changing the URL.

---

## How to test

Please go through the steps in order. Use a desktop/laptop first, then check it on your phone.

### Step 1 — Open the site cold

Visit the homepage without any context:

- https://kristynazackova.com

Spend **30 seconds** on the page before scrolling. Then scroll through once at a normal pace.

### Step 2 — View all four versions

Open each of the four versions in a separate tab:

- https://kristynazackova.com/?version=a
- https://kristynazackova.com/?version=b
- https://kristynazackova.com/?version=c
- https://kristynazackova.com/?version=d

Spend a few minutes on each. Click around. Note which one you'd actually want to keep reading.

### Step 3 — Click into the details

On whichever version you prefer, please:

- Click into one or two **ventures** (ConversationLens, ThreadLift, Life Made Easy, 3D Space). Read the "why" and description.
- Visit the **Blog** (link in the Writing section). Open one post.
- Click the **MentorCruise** badge in the Mentorship section. Imagine you're a potential mentee — would you book a trial?
- Try the **Get in touch / Email** link.
- Click the **LinkedIn** and **X/Twitter** icons.

### Step 4 — Mobile check

Open the site on your phone. Scroll through the default version. Note anything that feels off — text too small, images cut off, buttons hard to tap, animations distracting, etc.

### Step 5 — Anything broken?

Note anything that:

- Doesn't load
- Looks wrong (overlapping text, weird spacing, broken images)
- Goes to the wrong place when clicked
- Feels slow or janky

## Questions to answer

Please answer in whatever format is easiest — a doc, voice memo, email, Slack message. Bullet points are great. Don't overthink it.

### First impressions (default version, before switching)

1. In one sentence, **what do you think this person does**?
2. What was the **first feeling** the site gave you (curious, confused, intrigued, skeptical, bored, impressed, etc.)?
3. What's the **first thing your eye landed on**?
4. Did anything make you want to **stop reading or leave**? If so, what?

### Comparing the four versions

5. **Rank** versions A, B, C, D from favorite to least favorite.
6. For your favorite — **what specifically works**? (tone, layout, visuals, copy, structure...)
7. For your least favorite — **what doesn't work**?
8. Is there a piece of one version you'd want to **steal** into another? (e.g. "I like A's hero with D's ventures section")

### Clarity & messaging

9. After reading the homepage, can you explain in your own words **what I do professionally**?
10. Is it clear that I'm **both a product leader and a founder**? Do those two identities work together or feel disjointed?
11. Does the **Ventures** section make sense? Are the project descriptions too long, too short, or about right?
12. Does the **Experience** section add value, or is it redundant given the Ventures section?

### Mentorship section (most important)

13. Did you **notice the Mentorship section**? Where on the page?
14. Is it clear **what I offer** as a mentor and **who it's for**?
15. As a fellow coach — does the mentorship offer feel **credible and well-positioned**? What would make you trust it more?
16. If you were a potential mentee, would you click the **MentorCruise badge** to learn more? Why or why not?
17. What's missing that would make someone actually **book a trial**? (Testimonials, specific outcomes, sample topics, pricing transparency, a personal video, something else?)
18. Should the mentorship section be **bigger / earlier on the page**, or is its current placement right?

### Tone & personality

19. Does the site feel like a **real person** or like a corporate template?
20. Three adjectives that describe the **vibe** of the site?
21. Is there anything that feels **off-brand for someone offering coaching/mentorship** — too cold, too technical, too salesy, too modest?

### Writing / Blog

22. Did you click through to the **blog**? If yes, did anything draw you in? If no, why not?
23. Does the writing feel like it **adds credibility**, or is it skippable?

### Trust & credibility

24. What on this site makes you **trust** me?
25. What's **missing** that would make you trust me more (as a mentee, as a hiring manager, or as a peer)?

### Mobile

26. How does the site feel **on your phone** vs. desktop? Anything broken or annoying?

### Anything else

27. If I could change **only one thing** based on your feedback, what should it be?
28. Anything you noticed that I didn't ask about? Bugs, typos, weird interactions, ideas?
